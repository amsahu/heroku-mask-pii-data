Mask PII data

It takes tabular data and masks PII data column wise. It can detect PHONE, EMAIL and US ADDRESS. It may falsely mask some of your data but it most probably will not miss any of the mentioned critical entity and that is the aim.

Steps to follow:
  1. Choose file (csv or xlsx). Your file must contain header, otherwise it will miss the first row to mask. Total data (row*column) must be less than or equal to 1000, otherwise it may throw timeout error
  2. Click "Submit" to check info
  3. Click "Mask and download" and wait for your file to download
